Nintendo Entertainment System

During its initial release which was in Japan, the now beloved NES was known as the Famicom being released July 15, 1983. It offered the oppurtunity to play popular arcade games like Donkey Kong without having to leave the house. Some of the most popular games of the NES which sparked its popularity in the US were Super Mario Brothers and The Legend of Zelda.
