Nintendo DS

The DS had two screens (a normal top screen and a touchscreen bottom screen) and a built-in microphone. The DS allowed for multiple Ds consoles to directly interact with one another. With the now-defunct Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection service, DS consoles could interact online.
