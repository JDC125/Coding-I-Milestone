Nintendo Switch OLED

The Nintendo Switch OLED built upon the already great and popular Nintendo Switch, and added more/better features. It has a larger screen, and much better graphics because of the state-of-the-art OLED screen. The kickstand on the back of the switch for tabletop play is much wider making it much sturdier.
